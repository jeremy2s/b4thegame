The Future is Not Here Yet. From his early days leading a multimedia lab at NASA Kennedy Space Center to his current work as director of the University of Central Florida’s Mixed Emerging Technology Integration Lab (METIL), David Metcalf has pioneered the future of learning with cutting-edge innovators around the world.

Houston, We Don't Have a Problem. Retired NASA Space Operations Chief Engineer, Heather McDonald, brings deep insights into training, leadership, and high-risk decision-making in space operations. As NASA’s first female Chief Engineer of the International Space Station, she led critical missions for the ISS and space launches, training astronauts and engineers to navigate life-or-death situations.

What Were We Arguing About? At work, differences can spark innovation — or deepen divides. Irish poet and conflict mediator Pádraig Ó Tuama invites us to imagine another way — using the creative power of poetry to navigate disagreement, build empathy, and foster reconciliation.
